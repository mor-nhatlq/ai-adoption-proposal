Ca 2 luong deu dat toi trang thai: toan bo team su dung AI Agent hieu qua trong cong viec hang ngay. Quy trinh duoc chuan hoa, do luong va cai tien lien tuc.
Greenfield — Uu diem
  • AI tham gia tu ngay 1 — khong co legacy debt
  • Setup chuan ngay — conventions, rules, memory tu dau
  • Toc do nhanh — AI hieu context tu dau, khong can "hoc lai"
  • Architecture AI-friendly — thiet ke phu hop AI tu dau
  • ~3-4 tuan de dat full AI workflow
Legacy — Luu y
  • AI can HIEU truoc — can thoi gian doc va map codebase
  • Setup dan dan — khong the ap dung 100% ngay
  • Backward compatibility — can than khong break existing features
  • Test coverage first — can safety net truoc khi refactor
  • ~8-10 tuan de dat full AI workflow
